My Favorite Music

Billie Eilish


bury a friend - Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish is a 16 year old singer and songwriter. She wrote her first song, Ocean Eyes, at age 13.
She is well known for her songs You Should See Me in a Crown, and When the Partys Over.
I love her music because it is very different then any other music I have ever heard. She also has a very wide
varitey of music, ranging from heavier songs, such as bury a friend , to slower songs such as
idontwannabeyouanymore .

Twenty One Pilots


Chlorine - Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots is a band that started in 2009, with their first album Twenty One Pilots .
In their music they talk about very important topics such as mental health and suicide, loss of loved ones,
and even love. Their music is very influential and has helped me, and thousands of others, through hard times.
They have become extremely popular in the past couple years, with hit songs such as Jumpsuit and HeavyDirtySoul

Dean


instagram - Dean

Dean is a South Korean R&B/HipHop artist. He has been active since 2013, and in 2015 made his
US debut with a song called I'm Not Sorry with Grammy Award winning artist, Eric Bellinger.
On March 2016, he released his first EP called 130 Mood: TRBL . On this EP were songs such as
Bonnie & Clyde, 21, and What 2 Do . Along with his Ep, he has around
25 singles, inluding instagram and Love (ft. syd) .

RM



moonchild - RM

RM, also known as Kim Namjoon, is a South Korean Rapper, who is also a member
of the group BTS. In the group, he debuted as the leader and lead rapper. He has published many works,
including his first mixtape in 2015 called RM, featuring songs such as Do You
and Joke. He recently came out with his 2nd mixtape, Mono with Forever Rain
and Moonchild
